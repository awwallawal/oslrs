Design Evaluation Framework

As you explore each design direction, consider these criteria to identify which approach best serves your users and brand goals:

‚úÖ Layout Intuitiveness

Does the information hierarchy match your priorities? Can users immediately understand what to do next?

‚úÖ Interaction Style

Which interaction style fits your core experience? Does it match user expectations for survey completion?

‚úÖ Visual Weight

Which visual density feels right for your brand? Is it spacious and calm, or efficient and data-focused?

‚úÖ Navigation Approach

Which navigation pattern matches user expectations? Is progress always visible? Can users orient themselves easily?

‚úÖ Component Usage

How well do the components support your user journeys? Are the right elements emphasized?

‚úÖ Brand Alignment

Which direction best supports your emotional goals? Does it feel like "Oyo State" with the right balance of authority and approachability?
