# APPENDIX E: LGA POPULATION PROJECTIONS

---

*This appendix presents population data for all 33 Local Government Areas of Oyo State, based on the 2006 National Population Census with projections to 2024 using National Population Commission (NPC) growth rates.*

---

## E.1 Methodology

Population projections are calculated using the compound growth formula:

**P(t) = P(0) × (1 + r)^n**

Where:
- P(0) = 2006 Census population
- r = Annual growth rate (2.8–3.0% for urban LGAs; 2.3–2.5% for semi-urban; 2.0–2.2% for rural LGAs)
- n = Number of years (18 years for 2024 projection)

Growth rates are assigned based on the urbanisation classification of each LGA, consistent with NPC methodology for inter-censal projections.
