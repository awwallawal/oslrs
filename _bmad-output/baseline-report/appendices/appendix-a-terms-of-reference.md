# APPENDIX A: TERMS OF REFERENCE — EXTRACT

---

## A.1 Engagement Title

**Production of State Skilled Labour Register**

## A.2 Commissioning Authority

Ministry of Trade, Industry, Investment and Cooperatives
Oyo State Government
Secretariat, Agodi, Ibadan

## A.3 Consultant

Chemiroy Nigeria Limited
Principal Consultant: Lawal Awwal

## A.4 Engagement Date

18 November 2025

## A.5 Duration

Six (6) months from date of engagement

## A.6 Deliverables

The Terms of Reference specify three (3) deliverables:

| # | Deliverable | Description | Status |
|---|------------|-------------|:------:|
| 1 | **Establishment of a Data Center** | Procurement and installation of data processing infrastructure at Ministry premises; establishment of connectivity and cloud hosting | ✓ **COMPLETE** |
| 2 | **Creation of the State Labour Register** | Design and execution of statewide enumeration exercise to create a digital register of skilled workers across all 33 LGAs | **IN PROGRESS** (methodology complete; field enumeration pending resource mobilisation) |
| 3 | **Training of Ministry/Government Officials** | Capacity building programme for Ministry staff in the operation, management, and utilisation of the Registry platform | **DESIGN COMPLETE** (delivery scheduled for Gate 3) |

## A.7 Scope of Work

The engagement scope encompasses:

1. Establishment of physical and digital infrastructure for data collection, storage, and processing
2. Design and validation of a survey instrument for skilled labour enumeration
3. Development of a digital platform for field data collection, registry management, and public skills marketplace
4. Recruitment, training, and deployment of field enumeration teams across all 33 LGAs
5. Execution of statewide field enumeration
6. Data quality assurance and registry population
7. Training of Ministry officials in registry operation and utilisation
8. Project documentation and formal handover

## A.8 Resource Requirements

The Terms of Reference specify that the following resources are required for Deliverable 2 (field enumeration):

- Field Enumerators: 3 per LGA × 33 LGAs = 99 enumerators
- Field Supervisors: 1 per LGA × 33 LGAs = 33 supervisors
- Mobile data collection devices
- Transportation and communication allowances
- Community sensitisation materials
