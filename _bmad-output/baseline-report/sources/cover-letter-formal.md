## Submission of the Pre-Field Survey Status Report, Oyo State Labour & Skills Registry

Honourable Commissioner,

We are pleased to submit the Pre-Field Survey Status Report for the Oyo State Labour & Skills Registry (OSLSR). The report has been prepared as part of our engagement with the Ministry to establish the State Electronic Labour Register, and to support the broader objective of improving access to skilled and formal labour for corporate organisations and businesses across Oyo State.

The report sets out, in full, the progress recorded across the six workstreams of the engagement, the field-survey readiness of the Registry platform, and the actions required from the Ministry to authorise the start of fieldwork.

Five points summarise our position at the date of this letter:

1. The Registry platform has been established and equipped to international standards.
2. The Labour Data Centre is operational and secured. Operator access is identity-protected, public traffic is shielded at the edge, and audit logs are immutable to support compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Act, 2023.
3. The survey instrument has been refined through two pre-field enumeration cycles and is now at Version 3, ready for the field-survey phase.
4. Capacity-building materials for the field staff (thirty-three Supervisors and ninety-nine Enumerators across the thirty-three Local Government Areas of the State) are in production for delivery in the days immediately preceding fieldwork.
5. The Build, Operate, Transfer model that governs this engagement is described in Section 7 of the report. The Transfer Protocol has been drafted and is in legal review ahead of execution by both parties.

Section 8 of the report sets out a small number of action items that require the Ministry's attention to authorise fieldwork and take the engagement into its next phase. None of these items requires complex inter-Ministerial coordination, and all of them can be addressed in a single working session at the Honourable Commissioner's convenience.

We would be grateful for the Ministry's confirmation of the field-survey commencement date, together with the related authorisations described in the report. We are available for any clarification, briefing, or additional information the Ministry may require.
