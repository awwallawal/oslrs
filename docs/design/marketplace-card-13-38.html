The trust system

two slots, never fighting

Government verified

Slot 1 · Verification pill

Top-right, at most one, by precedence: Government verified → Member-verified → none. Answers how independently verified.

Member-verified

Earned, not assumed

Appears only once the person confirms their own record via the magic-link / Assessor callback. Ghost rows never earn it.

[Association] — confirmed member

Slot 2 · Provenance line

Who vouches. Shown for any association-sourced worker, additive to the pill. Never says a bare “Verified.” Long names truncate; full text on hover.

  • Mastery up front. Years-at-trade is often the strongest trust signal a worker has — promoted to a hero stat, with a ★ “seasoned” cue at 20+ years. Honest: it's derived from the number, not a claim.
  • No names, no photos. Scraper defence + privacy — the card is profession-led; full profile & contact sit behind employer login. Trade-glyph avatars give warmth with zero PII.
  • Future-proof. If Government relaxes the photo rule, the Story 9-12 magic-link self-update flow already exists — reach each worker by email/phone to add a photo. No new infrastructure.
  • Build it once. TradeAvatar and TrustBadge should be shared components — the same person looks the same on the card, the profile page, and the registry table.
